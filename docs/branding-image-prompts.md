# GPT-Image-2 Branding And Site Asset Prompts

Use this as a sidecar package for generating the visual system, hero asset, and supporting graphics for Tony Wu's personal homepage. The current homepage graphic should be treated as a placeholder direction only. The next visual pass should feel like an editorial technical artifact from a serious systems engineering practice, not a decorative dashboard.

Keep final readable text, navigation labels, project names, and accessibility copy in the website code. Generated images should supply mood, artifact language, texture, diagrams, and visual hierarchy.

## Brand Thesis

Tony Wu is a Vancouver-based systems engineering practitioner working across rail and transit, MBSE, full lifecycle systems engineering, assurance evidence, AI-assisted engineering practice, INCOSE Canada leadership, and the INCOSE Technical Leadership Institute.

The brand should communicate:

- Practical systems thinking for complex delivery.
- Rail and infrastructure seriousness without literal train marketing.
- Evidence, interfaces, readiness, and decision memory.
- A public body of practice: papers, field notes, lab experiments, and professional society contribution.
- Future independent optionality without looking like an active consulting sales funnel.
